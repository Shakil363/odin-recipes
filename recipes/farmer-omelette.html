Farmer's Omelette

A hearty Farmer's Omelette recipe

A Farmer's Omelette is a hearty dish with eggs, sautéed potatoes, bell peppers, onions, and cheese. Cooked in a skillet, it’s flavorful and filling, perfect for breakfast or brunch, and garnished with fresh herbs for extra flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • 1 medium potato, diced
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper (red or green), diced
  • 1/4 cup onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup mushrooms, sliced (optional)
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar or Swiss cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh herbs like parsley or chives (for garnish)
  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil

Steps:

  1. In a non-stick skillet over medium heat, add a little butter or oil and cook diced potatoes until tender and golden, about 5-7 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add more butter or oil if needed. Sauté the bell pepper, onion, and mushrooms until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add the potatoes back to the skillet, then pour the egg mixture over the veggies.
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top and cook until eggs are set and cheese is melted, about 3-5 minutes. Fold if desired.
  6. Garnish with fresh herbs and enjoy.
